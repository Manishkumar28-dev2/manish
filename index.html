    FAQ'S

    • Nike Points is a loyalty program that allows customers to earn points with each purchase, which can later be redeemed for exclusive rewards, discounts, and early access to limited-edition products.
    • Points are earned on purchases made through Nike's website, app, and in-store.
    • You can earn Nike Points through the following:
    • Every dollar spent on eligible purchases earns points.
    • Participating in special Nike events or promotions.
    • Referring friends to Nike’s app or website.
    • Bonus points are sometimes available during promotions or by purchasing specific collections.
    • Points can be redeemed for rewards including:
    • Discounts on future purchases.
    • Access to limited-edition or early-release products.
    • Free shipping and other perks.
    • Simply log into your Nike account, view your points balance, and select available rewards in the "Rewards" section.
    • To track your points:
    • Log in to your Nike account.
    • Go to the "Rewards" or "My Account" section where your points balance is displayed.
    • You can also check point expiration dates and upcoming rewards.
